# DePauw University

> DePauw University is a private liberal arts university located in Greencastle, Indiana, founded in 1837. It offers a rigorous co-curricular liberal arts education emphasizing critical thinking, leadership, and real-world experience through small classes, strong faculty mentorship, and robust experiential learning opportunities.

DePauw University has been preparing students for lives of meaning and purpose for more than 180 years. The university is nationally recognized for innovation, career outcomes, and its commitment to meeting full demonstrated financial need for incoming students.

## Academics

> DePauw offers 75+ majors, minors, and pathways across four academic divisions. Class sizes are small — the vast majority of courses enroll fewer than 30 students, with no classes of 50 or more.

### Academic Schools and Colleges

- [College of Liberal Arts and Sciences](https://www.depauw.edu/academics/clas/): The core liberal arts and sciences division offering programs across humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences
- [The Creative School](https://www.depauw.edu/academics/creativeschool/): Focused on arts, media, communication, and design disciplines
- [School of Business and Leadership](https://www.depauw.edu/academics/sbl/): Offers business, finance, analytics, and leadership programs
- [The Institute of Music](https://www.depauw.edu/academics/music/): A nationally recognized conservatory-style music school within a liberal arts university
